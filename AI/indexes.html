AI Assistant

An AI Assistant is a digital tool powered by artificial intelligence designed to perform tasks, provide information, and assist users in various ways. It can respond to voice or text commands, helping with activities such as setting reminders, managing schedules, answering questions, or providing real-time customer support. AI assistants are commonly used in devices like smartphones, smart speakers, and websites, offering tailored and efficient assistance in areas such as healthcare, virtual task management, and customer service. Their ability to learn and adapt over time makes them increasingly versatile and integral to modern life.

Voice Assistants

These respond to spoken commands to perform tasks like setting reminders, playing music, or answering questions.

Virtual Assistants

Designed to help with digital tasks like managing schedules, sending emails, and organizing files.

Customer Service Assistants

Provide real-time support on websites, answering FAQs, and resolving customer queries.

Healthcare Assistants

Help in diagnosing symptoms, scheduling appointments, and managing health records.
